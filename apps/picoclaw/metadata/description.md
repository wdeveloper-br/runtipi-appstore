# PicoClaw

**Tiny, Fast, and Deployable anywhere — automate the mundane, unleash your creativity.**

PicoClaw is an ultra-efficient personal AI assistant written in Go. It's designed to be ultra-lightweight and highly compatible, running seamlessly on everything from a $10 Sipeed Maixcam to high-end servers.

### ✨ Key Features

- **🚀 Ultra-Fast:** Boots in milliseconds and performs up to 400x faster startup than traditional assistants.
- **🐜 Tiny Footprint:** Consumes less than 10MB of RAM, making it perfect for resource-constrained environments.
- **🔌 Multi-Provider Support:** Connect to various LLM backends including OpenAI, DeepSeek, Volcengine, and Zhipu.
- **💬 Universal Channels:** Use PicoClaw via Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, Slack, Feishu, and more.
- **🛠️ Extensible Skills:** Automate tasks with built-in skills for web searching, scheduling, and custom workflows.

### 📦 Purpose

PicoClaw acts as a versatile bridge between powerful AI models and your favorite communication tools, allowing you to build a personalized, automated assistant that is always ready to help without the overhead.
